Welcome
The Nakuru Happy Church of Pipeline is a Christ-centered, church located in Nakuru, Pipeline,along Nakuru-Nairovi Highway.

Our Ministry
We proclaim that Jesus is:
Our Savior (Acts 4:12); Our Sanctifier (1 Corinthians 1:30); Our Healer (James 5:15); Our Coming King (Acts 1:11).
The mission of our church is to contribute to the development of strong Christian communities everywhere. Help people to hear the Gospel of Salvation, to know about Jesus Christ, to believe in God and firmly believe in God, to be transformed by God, to be empowered by the Holy Spirit to live in obedience, live in love, live in service, live in joy… in the great name of God.
