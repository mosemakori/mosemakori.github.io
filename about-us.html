Our History
The Nakur Happy Church of Pipeline, Nakuru began from a small number of families who loved Jesus Christ. Through earnest prayer and God’s will, this church was officially recognized on November 12, 2010 by the NCCK. In 2014, God blessed the church with a temporal building where we could congregate. However, we wanted a place where we could freely worship and seek the Lord - an established church of our own. For many years, we relentlessly prayed for God to provide us with a church. Many times we thought we had found a church, only to be met with hardships. Nonetheless, we continued to trust in God’s plan for this church. On September 2016, the Lord provided a place to establish our church firmly in the community. After many difficulties with financing, planning, and remodeling, the Nakuru Happy Church had a place of worship on February 2, 2017. We are forever grateful to the God who constantly loves, provides, and delivers his people.
